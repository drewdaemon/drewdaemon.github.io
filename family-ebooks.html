How to read the ebooks

These books are distributed as EPUB files. EPUB is a file format for ebooks.

E-readers such as Kindle support EPUB. To get a book on your Kindle, visit the send-to-Kindle page.

To read an EPUB (or any ebook for that matter) on your phone or desktop computer, you need a computer program installed.

You can download the EPUB and try to open it to see if you already have a reader installed. Macs, for example, come with an application called "Books" that understands EPUB files.

If you find you need to install a reader, Amazon's Kindle Reader software is very good and can be installed on Android, iPhone, Mac, and Windows. It also syncs your library between all your devices.

Calibre is another good choice for desktop computers. It isn't as pretty, but still does a good job and has no ads.
